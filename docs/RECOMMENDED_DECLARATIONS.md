# Recommended Declaration Text

The manuscript's current declaration wording is substantively sufficient for BMC Ophthalmology submission. The recommendations below are optional enhancements for additional transparency.

## Current manuscript wording (as of 2026-04-14)

### Availability of data and materials
The datasets used and/or analysed during the current study are available from the corresponding author on reasonable request, subject to institutional and ethical regulations regarding patient data confidentiality. A peer-review code package containing the analysis scripts, input specifications, environment requirements, and representative outputs is provided as Additional file 2 and will be deposited in a public DOI-minting repository before publication.

### Ethics approval and consent to participate
This study was approved by the Clinical Research Ethics Committee of Izmir City Hospital (No. 2026/337). Approval for the secondary analysis of anonymised archival data was obtained before extraction for the present research analysis, although the underlying clinical examinations had accrued between September 2022 and October 2025. The study adhered to the tenets of the Declaration of Helsinki, and the requirement for written informed consent was waived because of the retrospective design.

### Consent for publication
Not applicable.

## Optional enhanced wording (if editor requests more detail)

### Availability of data and materials (enhanced)
The raw Pentacam export files and linked hospital-record variables used in this study contain potentially identifiable patient information and are therefore not publicly available. The current transparency package includes the analysis code, figure-generation scripts, data schema, and de-identified aggregate outputs supporting the reported results. Access to the underlying patient-level data may be considered by the corresponding author on reasonable request, subject to approval by the Clinical Research Ethics Committee of Izmir City Hospital and institutional data-protection requirements.

### Consent for publication (enhanced)
Not applicable. No individual participant details, images, or other identifying material are reported.
